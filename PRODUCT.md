# VibeStack

## Register
brand

## Purpose
Help people who use ChatGPT daily but haven't built anything with AI yet discover open-source tools and learn to vibe code. Level up their career by going from "I chat with AI" to "I build with AI."

## Target Users
- Non-technical professionals stuck in their role wanting to move into AI/tech
- Founders prototyping without a dev team
- Designers, marketers, PMs who want to ship, not just spec
- Developers who want to move 10x faster with AI

## Product
A curated directory of open-source tools for vibe coders, organized by skill level (Prompter, Tweaker, Builder). Each tool has a profile page with plain-English explanations, real-world examples, dev process context, and community demos from X.

## Brand Personality
Warm creative friend. Like a friend who texts you "look what I found." Approachable, encouraging, inclusive. Not techy-bro, not corporate, not lecture-y. Talks to you like a person, not a developer.

## Anti-references
- Dark mode hacker aesthetic
- Purple gradient AI slop
- Dense technical dashboards
- Bro-culture tech sites
- Generic SaaS templates

## Design References
- Notion: warm, editorial, generous whitespace
- Are.na: creative, curated, thoughtful
- Product Hunt: card-based discovery with social proof

## Design Principles
1. **Warm over techy.** Light mode, soft tones, friendly copy. The audience includes people who've never used a terminal.
2. **Simple over feature-dense.** Every element earns its place. If in doubt, remove it.
3. **Show, don't tell.** Visual demos, real examples, analogies. Not technical documentation.
4. **Inclusive by default.** Vibe coders are diverse. No assumptions about gender, background, or technical level.
5. **Career-oriented.** Everything connects back to real outcomes: getting hired, promoted, funded, or unblocked.

## Accessibility
WCAG AA minimum. High contrast text. 44px touch targets. Keyboard navigable. Semantic HTML.
